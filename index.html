About me.

I’ve been coding for over five years, beginning my journey in 2020. Initially, I contributed to various university projects, working with Python, C++, and C. During this time, I developed a strong foundation in programming fundamentals and problem-solving skills.

As I delved deeper, I discovered my passion for data science, machine learning, and data engineering. I studied optimization, data pipelines, and the art of crafting data products to meet real business needs. I’m currently working as a Machine Learning Engineer, where I apply these skills to projects impacting more than 100 million customers at Nubank. My focus is on designing and deploying robust ML solutions that drive tangible results.
