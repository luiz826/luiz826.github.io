About Me

My journey from coding fundamentals to large-scale data engineering.

I've been coding for over five years, beginning my journey in 2020. Initially, I contributed to various university projects, working with Python, Scala, C++, and C. During this time, I developed a strong foundation in programming fundamentals and problem-solving skills.

As I delved deeper, I discovered my passion for data science, machine learning, and data engineering. I studied optimization, data pipelines, and the art of crafting data products to meet real business needs.

I'm currently working as a Data Scientist III (MLOps) at Cloudwalk, the fastest growing fintech in the world, where I create, deploy, and maintain credit models and help build the model deployment platform. Previously, I worked at Nubank, contributing to projects that reached more than 100 million customers. My focus is on designing and operating robust ML solutions that drive tangible results.
